Biography

Professor Shakro Tetunashvili is a highly respected Georgian mathematician and Full Professor at the Georgian Technical University, specializing in Harmonic Analysis and Orthogonal Series with significant contributions to the theory of Universal Function Series.

Professor Tetunashvili is a prominent figure in mathematics, renowned for his significant contributions to harmonic analysis, orthogonal series, and related areas.

Research Interests & Contributions

Professor Tetunashvili's research focuses on the following key areas:

  • Harmonic Analysis
  • Orthogonal Series
  • Functional Analysis

His significant contributions include research on:

  • Theory of Universal Function Series: Presented on this topic at the International Congress of Mathematicians (ICM) in Rio de Janeiro (2018).
  • Uniqueness Theorems: For various types of function series, including trigonometric, Walsh, and general orthogonal series.
  • Summability Methods with Variable Order: Particularly in the context of Fourier series.
  • Reconstruction of Coefficients of Multiple Trigonometric Series: Even for Lebesgue non-integrable sums.
